What does it take to be an astronaut?

Next Launch Date

August 19, 2025

Trip Duration

6 months

To begin the process, we ask you to review these application requirements prior to starting your application. These requirements were put in place by Mars The Project Department of Health & Human Services in order to ensure your safety and comfort during spaceflight.

General Requirements

  • Minimum age of 21 years old
  • Standing height between 62 and 75 inches (5'2" - 6'3")
  • Be able to pass the Mars long-duration flight astronaut physical

Physical Requirements

  • Free from disease or psychiatric disorders
  • Free from drug, alcohol, or tobacco dependency
  • Normal range of motion and functionality in all joints
  • Age- and gender-adequate fitness level
  • Blood pressure no higher than 140/90 measured in a sitting position
  • Visual acuity in both eyes of 20/20 (100%), either naturally or with corrective lenses

Application Fee

The Mars The Project application fee is $55. This non-refundable fee is paid electronically through the Mars application portal.
